Breaking: Local Archer Destroys Desert

Explosive Reunion of BFFs

By John Fate

In a dramatic case of "Long time, no see", a sparring match between the Heroic Spirit identified as Gilgamesh, the King of Heroes, and the Divine Spirit identified as Enkidu, the Chains of Heaven, reduced an entire desert to a flaming crater.

Witnesses were awestruck by the sight of hundreds of Noble Phantasms clashing in the sky, followed by a massive explosion that was visible from outer space.

When asked for a comment, the King of Heroes responded with,"Get your worthless cameras out of my face before I reduce you mongrels to dust."

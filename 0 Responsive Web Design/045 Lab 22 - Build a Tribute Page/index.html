Chadwick Boseman

Gone, but not forgotten

An inspiration to us all:

Despite his terminal cancer diagnosis, Chadwick chose to keep his illness private, giving the world one final performance as T'Chala in Avengers: Endgame.

Till his final moments, he did not allow his illness to define him or hold him back.

Boseman himself was inspired to reprise his role as T'Chala despite his illness, when he visited a hospital and learned that two children were looking forward to T'Chala's return to the big screen.
